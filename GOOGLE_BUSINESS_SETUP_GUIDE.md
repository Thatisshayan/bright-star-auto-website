Photos are crucial. They build trust and get the most engagement:

### Business Photos (Add 5-10)

## Troubleshooting

**"Visit Website" button not showing**
- Re-enter website URL and save
- Wait 24-48 hours
- Make sure profile is 80%+ complete

**Photos not displaying**
- Use JPG or PNG format
- Minimum 250x250 pixels
- Upload through official Google Business interface

**Reviews not appearing**
- Profile must be verified
- Reviewers need Google accounts

**Business address incorrect**
- Edit in Business Info and verify with Google

**Customers can't call**
- Check phone number includes area code
- Verify no formatting errors
